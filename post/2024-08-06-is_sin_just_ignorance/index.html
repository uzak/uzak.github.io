Is sin just ignorance?

August 06, 2024  

src

  • Why do we fall from this blessed state of freedom into this imprisonment we call life. Death by self-imprisonment. We take it as normal. We look down, we don’t look up. The unlimited sky is always there.
  • View from airplane offers best contrast. Problems dissolved - unlimited expansion.
  • Sin is when things go wrong.
  • Mistaken identity. All problems of the world arise from it.
  • Repentance is turning back to freedom again.
  • Unselfishness, you think less about me.
  • We’re all mini Christs. We have to take our cross.
  • It seems I would not say reasonable, but wonderful.
  • Where is that end where spirit, freedom begins?
  • Whatever is happening is contained in this unchanging, ever present, presence of God.
  • You and me and this, what is called the third point. Three - basic building components of all religions. Duality contained in the third.
  • The only problem with duality is that it is in conflict.
  • The duality is only problematic when we forget the third point.
  • Come back to simplicity. Distrust of complexity. Common sense.
  • This present is asking nothing of us, it accepts you as we are. Forgiveness - prime feature of this presence. Most merciful. Forgiving.
  • Where do all these judgements come from? Not coming from user selves?
  • Sin is mistaken identity. Sin is ignorance.
  • Perhaps duality is ignorance of the one.
