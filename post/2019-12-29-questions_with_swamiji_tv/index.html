Questions with Swamiji (TV)

December 29, 2019  

link

  • Goal: knowledge.
  • Quality: simplicity.
  • Have self-confidence. Be punctual, it shows you are earnest about it.
  • Life is relationships.
  • Try to enrich every relationship with love. Don’t put reciprocity, expectations in place.
  • Seek love which is God. Seek freedom (psychological freedom from expectations, from seeking advantage) so you can discover that love.
  • Love is unity in diversity. If diversity is real, there is no love.
  • In non-duality the truth is one but appears as many. Differences but not divisions. We are all one in essence but different in appearance.
  • You are able to overlook the outward differences because you focus on the inner source which is the same in all living beings.
  • In dualistic ethics there is no scope for forgiveness. How can you forgive real evil? Rama, Krishna, Buddha, Christ were teaching non-dualistic ethics, forgiving your enemies.
