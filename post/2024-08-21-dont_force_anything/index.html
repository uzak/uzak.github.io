  • Life in the present

  • Understand the impermanence of life
  • Power of acceptance
    • doesn’t mean surrender
    • face every situation with calmness and clarity
  • Letting go
  • Mindfulness
    • Marcus Aurelius (MA): happiness of your life depends on the quality of your thoughts
    • MA: you have the power over your mind, not outside effects
  • Embrace the silence
  • Enjoy the journey, not just the outcome
  • Find joy in simplest things
    • MA: very little is needed to make a happy life, it is all within your self, in your way of thinking
    • The richest person is not the one who has the most, but who needs least - learns gratitude
  • The art of gratitude
    • MA: when you arise in the morning, think of what precious privilege it is to live, to breathe, to enjoy, to love.
    • Let gratitude to be your guiding principle to a peaceful and happy life
  • Appreciate solitude
    • You can discover endless inspiration and peace
