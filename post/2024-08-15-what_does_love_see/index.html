What does love see?

August 15, 2024  

src

  • Prayer is raising of consciousness where you transcend this world of death
  • prayer is reaching up into this (points higher)
  • one day it will become more real to you than the world of death down here
  • It’s no use to save the world if we are the cause of the trouble
  • The hope is that in spirit you realize the existence of greater life where is no death.
  • Do you meet a lot of realized people? I’ve never met anybody. I would not claim that myself fully, partially at times.
  • Once you start working with spirit there is no need for audience.
  • Spirit doesn’t need public acclaim, his work are unseen, unknown. He’s the real invisible power behind the scenes. It’s the life, the life force. Who’s ever seen it? Who’s ever seen electricity? It’s recognized by its effects.
  • John the baptist: Behold, the lamb of god that takes away the sin of the world.
  • When the heart is melted, what comes out of it? Love. No glorification, no I love you.
  • What does love see? Love sees perfection in the bellowed? Love see no ill. Nothing but loveliness. Love sees purity. The pure in heart see god.
  • There is no need to go to India to look for gurus. Look at the lamb, let your heart be melted, let your tears run and you’ll be as close to god as you ever be. You don’t need a teacher. Teacher is in-front of your nose.
  • My entire life has expressed not what I’m, but what I’m not.
  • You want peace? Stillness? Earth is still, rocks are still. Put your feet on the ground. But the mind likes its own restlessness.
  • If you want to bring the world to peace, star with yourself. That’s the essence of prayer. Heal yourself.
  • The destiny of the world of sin is to rot away. Sin is death. Duality is on death row, duality is death.
  • Real life is in spirit.
  • If you’re serious about helping yourself or even better helping others, put prayer into practice. Practice what you preach. To make whole be whole.
  • Humanism - worship of the body. We think we’re so clever. We completely turn away from the point of life. We miss the true destiny of man - which is spirit, which is the kingdom of heaven.
