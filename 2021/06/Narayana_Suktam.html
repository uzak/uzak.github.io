source

  • deva - shining. The one light that is always shining: consciousness.
  • Happiness flowing from (coffee) cup into heart -> revert.
  • By conditioning gold as neckless you have not made its value lesser.
  • Gold is the origin of necklace and gold abides in the necklace.
  • Existence has an opposite - non existence. Being has none. Being is the foundation of the entire universe. Being is atma, the essence of entire universe of forms.
  • Parayanam - supreme goal to be reached by devotees.
  • Whatever is nama rupa is not tattvam.
  • Isvara is tattva of all tattvas, supreme tattva.
  • Who begins near goes far. Who begins far does not go anywhere.
  • sat = cit, cit = sat. What you know exists.
  • Smallness of the pot is attributed to akasha which is not small.
  • Difference between chaos and cosmos is rtam and satyam.
  • Purusha is reddy all over the body except black/blue neck.
