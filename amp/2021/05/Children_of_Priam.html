Children of Priam

May 16, 2021

Priam (priya):

  • Hector - from “to have, to hold; to check, to restrain”. Represents security - artha. Eldest child of Priam. Good father and husband. After killing him, Achilles attached his body to his chariot.
  • Paris - slayer of Achilles (peoples’ sorrow is killed by kama); “barge” (gr:baris). Chose Aphrodite among goddesses because she promised him the most beautiful woman in the world. Thus caused the Troyan War by abducting Helene. Lacked courage and skill in battle. He preferred to use bow and arrow - not weapons of choice for the courageous.
  • Deiphobus - “fear”. Maybe the most cunning of Trojan princes, married Helen after Paris’ death. Killed by Menelaus (“vigor/rage/power of people”) after the fall of Troy.
  • Helenus - (Helene - “light”) The twin of Cassandra and, like her, a seer. Lost out to Deiphobus in competition for the hand of Helen after Paris’s death. Later marries Andromache.
  • Polydorus - “many gifted”. Youngest of the sons. Killed by King Polymestor of Thrace during or after the Fall of Troy.
  • Troilus - “little Tros” or “destroy Troy”. Possibly fathered by Apollo. Killed by Achilles.
  • Polites - “citizen”, “civilian”. During the passage of the Trojan Horse in the Trojan War, Polites was one of those who accepted the gift. During the fall of Troy and the attempted escape to Latium, Neoptolemus shot an arrow in Polites’ leg. Polites fell, escaping Neoptolemus, who pursued Polites to his father’s palace. Priam called on the gods to punish Neoptolemus, but in that scene, Priam was also killed by Neoptolemus. His killer was “a Greek Legend and the son of Achilles, the hero of the Greek army at Troy.”
  • Gorgythion - “blameless”. Killed in battle by Teucer, whose arrow was aimed at Hector. Gorgythion is referred to at his death as “…the brave son of Priam.
  • Agathon - last of Priam’s sons to be slain. Meaning: the good, the benevolent one - dharma.
  • Mestor - “adviser”, “counsellor”. Killed by Achilles.
  • Laodice (daughter of Priam) - people justice. Most beautiful of Priam’s daughters.
