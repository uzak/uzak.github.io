Ready Player One

Oct 17, 2020

The year is 2045 and the reality sucks so much that practically all humanity spends most of its time in a virtual reality called OASIS. Recently its Author has passed away and he’s offered his position including the ownership of OASIS to anybody who masters his three challenges:

  1. Win an unwinnable race. The key to this challenge is to drive backwards.
  2. Ask the Lady of the Author to dance. Easy, isn’t it? Well, the Lady is trapped in the “Overlook Hotel” from the horror movie “The Shinning” and she’s dancing with dead people. On the way to the Lady, one has to go through all the zombies.
  3. The final challenge takes place on the planet Doom. But this is not the challenge. It is to win an unknown game while playing it! How to do? Just like life, really. The key is to play without going for the win. And the name of the first game? “Adventure” . And the price obtained is the name of the Author of the game.

After mastering the three challenges the winner - Parzival - is presented with a huge golden “easter egg”. And what does the winner gain? Well, the usual - the world with the princess - of course.
