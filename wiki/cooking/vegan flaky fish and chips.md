## "Fillets"

    1x tofu
    1tbsp nori flakes
    6x toothpick

## Batter

    120x plain flour
    1/2 tsp salt
    1 tsp cracked black pepper
    2 tsp baking powder
    150ml ice cold water

## Chips
    
    600g potatoes
    oil
    salt

## Sauce tartare

    sour cream
    1 tbsp gherkin
    1 tsp lemon juice
    1 tbsp fresh parsley
    salt & pepper
