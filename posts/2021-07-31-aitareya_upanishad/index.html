Introduction

  • sharma, varma, gupta, dasa - titles added to the names of brahmanas, kshatriyas, vaishays and sudras.

Class 2: Mantra 1-1

  • Shantipatha:
    • Om vAn me manasi pratiSThitA - let my speech be established in my mind
    • mano me vAci pratiSThitam - let my mind be established in my speech
    • AvirAvIrma edhi
    • vedasya ma ANIsthah -reveal truth of Vedas to me
    • zrutam me mA prahAsIh, anenAdhItena ahorAtrAn sandadhAmi - may my learning not leave me any time. May my learning be bright and brilliant. I will study day and night (same as: saha vIryam karavavahi)
    • rtam vadiSyAmi
    • satyam vadiSyAmi - I speak the truth manifested, I live the truth
    • tanmAmavatu - may lord protect me
    • tadvaktAramavatu, avatu mAm avatu vaktAramavatu vaktAram - may Lord protect my teacher
    • om zAntih, zAntih, zAntih.
  • 3x shanti - to protect from adhiatmika, adhibautika, adhidaivika obstacles.
  • atma derivations
    • Apnoti {ap}. Vyapnoti. Pervades everything. Is not conditioned by space.
    • Atti {ad}. Eats everything. Enjoyer of all experiences with objects. Nature of consciousness, not conditioned by objects.
    • Attate. Not conditioned by time. Has eternal existence - no death. Supreme paramatma, free from Samsara, which is characteristic of worldly existence.
  • edhi - bhava
  • prajahAti {pra-Ha} - renounce

Class 3: Mantra 1-1

  • agre - before. Sristeh purvam. Prag stristeh.
  • Before creation it was in the form of paramatma. After in the form of jagat -> paramatma and jagat are one and the same. Two names because before sristhi it was in karana avasta and after in karya. Bheda is only in the avastha, not in the substance.
  • no svagata bheda (internal difference)
  • no sajatiya bheda (many different within a specie)
  • no vijaytiya bheda (species bheda)
  • cestha - effort
